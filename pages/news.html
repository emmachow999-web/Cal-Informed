The Future of Online Privacy: Should Tech Companies Be Regulated?

As concerns about online privacy continue to grow, there is an ongoing debate about whether tech companies should be regulated to protect consumers' personal data. The debate is particularly relevant in the context of the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and the potential for federal legislation to regulate online privacy.

The Ethics of Artificial Intelligence: Should AI Be Used to Monitor and Regulate Online Behavior?

The increasing use of artificial intelligence (AI) to monitor and regulate online behavior has raised ethical concerns about privacy, bias, and accountability.
