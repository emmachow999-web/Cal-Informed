California Age-Appropriate Design Code Act Takes Effect

As of July 1, 2026, the California Age-Appropriate Design Code Act requires companies to prioritize the safety and well-being of minors online, including implementing default privacy settings and providing clear information about data collection practices. This law affects companies like Meta and TikTok that operate in California. The goal is to protect kids from harmful online content and data misuse.
