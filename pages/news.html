The Tension Between Data Protection and National Security

The balance between data protection and national security is a longstanding debate, with many arguing that strong data protection laws can hinder law enforcement and intelligence agencies' ability to prevent and investigate crimes. Others contend that robust data protection is essential for preventing abuse of power and protecting human rights. The debate has intensified in recent years, with the rise of encryption and other technologies that can limit government access to personal data.
