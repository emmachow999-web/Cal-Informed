Your Rights Under California Law

6 Rights You Already Have

The CCPA and CPRA give every California resident these rights — regardless of age.

01

Right to Know

Ask any company what data they have about you.

02

Right to Access

Get a copy of your actual personal data.

03

Right to Delete

Request companies erase your data within 45 days.

04

Right to Opt Out

Stop companies from selling your data to advertisers.

05

Right to Correct

Fix inaccurate information companies hold about you.

06

Right to Non-Discrimination

Companies can't punish you for using your rights.
